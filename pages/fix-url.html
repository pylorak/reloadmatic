You can fix the reload URL to a certain value by entering it into the text field below. If you fix the URL this way, ReloadMatic will always reload this URL given by you, no matter where the webpage is currently at.

Note, that you cannot reissue forms (or other POST data in general) with a fixed URL. This may cause your pages to not work as expected.

Tip: If your URL redirects, you might want to enable Sticky Reload too to keep periodic reloading to your URL turned on after the redirect.
